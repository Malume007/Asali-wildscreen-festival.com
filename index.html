🌍 Welcome to Tabora – The Heart of Tanzania

Karibu Tabora!

A warm welcome to Tabora - Tanzania from Shomari Ibrahim, owner and founder of Asali Wild Screen Festival. A celebration of tasting honey, Asali walk a thon, Tours film, art, culture, and agriculture in the land of honey and tobacco.

Tabora, Tanzania’s largest region, is a land of golden savannas, ancient trade routes, and thriving farms. From the buzzing beehives of the Miombo woodlands to the historic streets once walked by explorers and traders, Tabora is a living story - and you are invited to experience it all.

🎬 About the Festival

Asali Wild Screen Festival is a one-of-a-kind event that unites tasting honey, tours film, business network, traditional arts, sustainable farming, and tourism. Each year, artists, farmers, filmmakers, and travelers gather in Tabora to explore creativity and culture through cinema, honey, and heritage.

🎥 Festival Highlights

🎞️ Film & Arts

  • Open-air film screenings under the stars.
  • Documentaries on nature & African identity.
  • Beeswax sculpture exhibitions.

🍯 Honey & Tobacco Tours

  • Guided visits to local beekeeping farms.
  • Discover tobacco cultivation process.
  • Sustainable farming techniques.

🎭 Traditional Arts & Culture

  • Nyamwezi, Yege, Swezi and Sukuma dances.
  • Drumming & storytelling evenings.
  • Urambo Museum & Livingstone’s Tembe.

🌳 Nature & Adventure

  • Birdwatching safaris.
  • Cycling through farmlands.
  • Night bonfire folklore sessions.
