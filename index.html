🌍 About The Festival

Karibu Tabora! The Heart of Tanzania

A warm welcome from Shomari Ibrahim, Founder & Visionary of Asali Wild Screen Festival. We are a celebration of tasting pure Tabora Honey, the iconic Asali Walkathon, Film Screenings under African Stars, and celebrating Art, Culture, and Sustainable Agriculture.

Our mission is to put Tabora on the global map as a destination for culture, creativity, and community.
